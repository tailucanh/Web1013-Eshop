Product Description

Legendary fabric. Classic Henley style. Easygoing comfort. Why not check all the boxes with a Hanes® BeefyT® Men’s Long Sleeve Henley? Crafted from the famous heavyweight cotton fabric that set the standard for t-shirt quality a half-century ago, Hanes Henleys for men boast the same sturdy details as the iconic Hanes t-shirts: Double-needle stitching, high-density stitch count, and shoulder-to-shoulder taping. What’s new is a three-button placket, traditional round collarless neckline, and long sleeves. So soft, you will want to wear it on repeat, these classics look great paired with jeans, khakis, or shorts.
